Overview of REDF and its objectives

Introduction

The Real Estate Development Fund (REDF) was established by Royal Decree No. M/23 on 11/06/1394H (01/07/1974G), and is organizationally linked to the National Development Fund.

REDF drives real estate development in Saudi Arabia by enabling beneficiaries to own suitable housing through financing solutions, programs, and initiatives that keep pace with developmental shifts and support the Housing Program under Saudi Vision 2030.

The Role of REDF

01

Providing Support and Financing

Supporting real estate loans for beneficiaries and providing loans and guarantees to real estate developers and financing institutions.

02

Developing Housing Products and Initiatives

Designing and offering innovative financing programs and solutions targeting all citizen segments, focusing on middle and low-income groups to enable homeownership in cooperation with financial institutions.

03

Establishing Partnerships

Building strategic alliances with the public and private sectors to enhance the efficiency of the housing market and ensure the sustainability of financing solutions.

04

Supporting the Beneficiary Journey

Providing integrated digital services and certified consultations through the Real Estate Advisor platform, facilitating a seamless digital ownership journey.

05

Supporting Housing Stability

Enabling families to obtain suitable housing, which supports economic and social growth and positively impacts the quality of life.

Real Estate Development Fund

Empowering and enhancing the growth of the Saudi housing market through innovative and sustainable financing solutions.
