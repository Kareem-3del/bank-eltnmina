The Strategic Framework

The Real Estate Development Fund is one of the main drivers in supporting the real estate financing market, driving the real estate development wheel in the Kingdom, and its role has been embodied in implementing ambitious plans that respond to the growing housing needs of beneficiaries. The Royal Decree No. (m/47) dated 1442/05/23 has marked a pivotal station in the Fund's journey, as it approved the amendment to its basic system, enabling it to transcend the traditional expansion concept, towards working on sustaining support, and active participation in overall real estate growth, and in the housing sector in particular.
With the acceleration of the Kingdom's steps towards achieving Vision 2030 targets, the Fund has developed its strategy to be based on four main pillars that outline its future role, and determine its objectives through precise measurement indicators, where the Real Estate Development Fund's strategy was approved by the Board of Directors of the National Development Fund in 2025, reflecting the updated objectives of the Fund in line with Saudi Vision 2030, and included these pillars: financing and enabling the real estate sector, private sector participation, flexibility and financial strength, and excellence and institutional efficiency. The Fund is currently planning the phased implementation of this strategy to achieve the desired objectives.
The strategy includes six main objectives and four initiatives: beneficiaries, improving financing experience, developing innovative solutions, and maximizing the impact resulting from support programs, by expanding the scope of benefits to include a larger segment of citizens and developers. Through this integrated strategic path, the Fund looks forward to contributing an active role in raising the citizens' homeownership rate to 70% by 2030, while continuing to develop its operational efficiency and consolidating its financial sustainability, ensuring the continuity of impact and giving in the housing sector.

The Institutional Strategy Includes

Our Vision

Enabling and enhancing the growth of the Saudi housing market through providing sustainable and innovative financial solutions for citizens and participating parties in the market.

Our Mission

Enhancing citizens' homeownership rate through providing innovative and financially sustainable solutions for citizens and participating parties in the market.
