Corporate Strategy

Introduction

The Real Estate Development Fund was established by Royal Decree No. 23/M dated 11/06/1394 AH corresponding to 01/07/1974 CE. It began with the mission to support urban development in the Kingdom by enabling citizens to own homes through financing solutions that keep pace with demographic growth and development goals, while advancing housing targets under Saudi Vision 2030.

Vision

Enabling and strengthening the growth of the Saudi housing market through sustainable and innovative financial solutions for citizens and market participants.

Mission

Enhancing homeownership among citizens by providing innovative, financially sustainable solutions for citizens and market participants.

Message from the Chairman

The year 2025 marked the start of implementing the updated corporate strategy of the Real Estate Development Fund, designed to enable the growth of the Saudi housing market through innovative and sustainable financing solutions for citizens and market participants.

By the end of the year, the national homeownership rate reached 66.2%, making steady progress toward the Vision 2030 target of 70%.

The Kingdom is experiencing a generational shift toward a younger urban population, with more first-time buyers, singles, and young families entering the housing market. Homeownership is increasingly seen not only as an aspiration, but as a foundation for economic participation, stability, and long-term wellbeing.

In 2025 alone, more than 83,000 beneficiaries received support amounting to 52.7 billion. Since 2017, more than 920,000 beneficiaries have achieved homeownership through the Fund’s programs, with total financing exceeding 613 billion.
