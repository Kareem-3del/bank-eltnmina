## UI suggestion

Pull the 4 stat-banner messages out as standalone scroll-triggered "interstitial" sections between major content areas. They make the page feel like the printed magazine. Each banner:
- Full-bleed teal background
- Big number with a counter animation
- Short caption beneath

These are the strongest "wow" moments in the report — use them as content punctuation, not as nav targets.
