## Section 1 — Board Composition

**Title:** Board of Directors Composition

**Intro:**
> The REDF Board of Directors consists of ten members, representing several relevant entities as well as specialized experts.

**Diagram nodes (radial / org chart):**

- Chief Executive Officer of the REDF — Member
