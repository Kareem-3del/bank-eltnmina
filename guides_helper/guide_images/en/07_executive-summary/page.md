## Section 1 — Strategy Update

> The REDF has updated its strategy based on four main pillars aligned with Saudi Vision 2030. These pillars include real estate sector financing, private sector participation, enhancing financial strength, and institutional excellence. The REDF is currently implementing this strategy in phases to achieve its strategic objectives.
